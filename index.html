Arctic Ocean

The League was proactive in anticipating 2018 threats from a presidential administration focused on oil extraction despite the immense risks posed to whales, seals and Alaska's coastal communities.
  • Over 50 House and Senate meetings during Wilderness Week. 2017 advocating for Arctic Ocean bill cosponsorship
  • Worked with Sen. Markey / Menendez to oppose revising the 2017-2022 5 Year Oil and Gas program by getting 27 Senators to sign a letter and 18 cosponsors on new legislation
  • Delivered 19,000 member comments opposed .to new offshore drilling in the Arctic Ocean during the 5-year plan comment period

America's Largest Rainforest

By educating key members of Congress, organizing Alaska / DC coalitions and highlighting bad legislative initiatives as attacks to public lands, the League mobilized public support for Tongass and Chugach National Forest protections and promoted sustainable management of forest resources.
  • 49 Hill meetings to educate members of Congress and their staff as well as House and Senate Committees on threats and upcoming activities
  • Led letter to the Hill from 82 groups opposing Tongass riders in Interior appropriations
  • Launched action alert on keeping public lands in the Tongass public - 9,000+ members took action
